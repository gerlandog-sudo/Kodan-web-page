# Kodan Software · Product Strategy

## Purpose
Kodan focuses on **Custom Development & Legacy Modernization**. It positions itself as a high-end engineering consultancy that bridges the gap between legacy systems and modern technical excellence.

## Users
- **CTOs/VPs of Engineering**: Looking for technical reliability and modernization strategies.
- **Enterprise Product Owners**: Needing high-performance custom software.
- **Tech-Forward Founders**: Seeking premium development partners.

## Tone & Voice
- **Technical Precision**: The brand speaks the language of code (brackets, slashes, mono fonts).
- **Futuristic & Professional**: A "High-Agency" aesthetic that suggests cutting-edge capability without being immature.
- **Authoritative**: Confident in its ability to handle complex modernization tasks.

## Register
- **Brand**: The design is the product. The website must showcase technical craft and aesthetic mastery.

## Strategic Principles
1. **Engineering as Art**: Code is not just functional; it is a visible part of the brand identity.
2. **Atmospheric Immersion**: Use 3D elements and spatial design to create a memorable professional experience.
3. **Frictionless Trust**: High contrast and clear typography communicate clarity of thought.
